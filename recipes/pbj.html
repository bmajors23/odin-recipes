PB&J

Description

This recipe is to create a delicious Peanut Butter and Jelly sandwhich. A quick easy snack that everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter (Crunchy or Creamy)
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Put two slices of bread on a plate
  2. Take a knife and spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Clean the knife and spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  4. Combine the two slices of bread with the peanut butter and jelly facing one another
  5. Enjoy!
