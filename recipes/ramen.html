Ramen

Description

This recipe is to create a tasty bowl of ramne. Simple? Yes. Delicious? Also yes.

Ingredients

  • Pack of Maruchan Ramen
  • Optional: Egg

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil on high to medium-high heat.
  2. Add noodles from ramen packet to boiling water
  3. Stir occassionally until noodles are soft
  4. Optional: Crack and add an egg
  5. Turn off stove and add seasoning packet from packaging
  6. Mix thoroughly
  7. Once mixed, pour ramen into bowl and enjoy!
